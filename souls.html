Malenia, Blade of Miquella

"I am Malenia, Blade of Miquella. And I have never known defeat."

Born as a twin to Miquella, Malenia is cursed with the Scarlet Rot from within. Despite her decaying body, she became the most fearsome swordswoman in the Lands Between, wielding her prosthetic golden blade with unmatched grace. Her legend reached its peak during the Shattering, where she fought General Radahn to a standstill.

Starscourge Radahn

"I am Radahn, the Starscourge. And I will not allow the stars to move."

The mightiest demigod of the Shattering, General Radahn learned gravity magic in Sellia so he could continue to ride his beloved horse, Leonard. He is famous for challenging the stars themselves and halting their movement to protect the world. Even after losing his mind to the Scarlet Rot, he continues to hold the very fate of the cosmos in place.

Artorias the Abysswalker

"Thou shalt perish in the darkness..."

Sir Artorias was one of the four legendary Knights of Gwyn. He ventured into Oolacile to face the spreading Abyss. In a final act of heroism, he gave his protective shield to his companion Sif, leaving his own left arm to be shattered. Though the Abyss eventually consumed his soul, his sacrifice delayed the fall of the world into shadow.

Great Grey Wolf Sif

The Great Grey Wolf Sif is the eternal guardian of Artorias' grave. Having survived the Abyss thanks to his master's sacrifice, Sif grew to a colossal size. He wields Artorias' Greatsword in his mouth, fighting with a tragic elegance. Sif does not fight out of malice, but to prevent any other adventurer from suffering the same dark fate that claimed his beloved friend.
