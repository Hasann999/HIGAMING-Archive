Maelle

Maelle, as one of the most determined and elegant members of Expedition 33, sets out to save the world that the Paintress erases every year. Her relentless fighting style, combined with her grace, makes her an indispensable part of the group. While seeking her own destiny, Maelle must face the shadows of the past, carrying the last light of hope. Every step she takes represents the cry and the will to survive of a people doomed to be erased. She is not just a warrior, but a rare golden symbol shining in the darkness.

Alicia

Alicia is known as one of the most mysterious and powerful figures of Expedition 33; her mastery is hidden not only in her weapons but in her strategic intelligence. She is a hero who risks everything to break the unjust cycle imposed on the world by the Paintress. With her silent but effective presence, she provides both strength and guidance to her comrades in difficult times. Her story is an epic narrative of rebirth amidst sacrifice and destruction. Every strike she makes is a decisive step toward bringing back the erased colors of the world.
